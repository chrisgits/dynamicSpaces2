currently specializes in staging vacant homes that are preparing to be listed for sale.

Many home owners are not aware of the benefits of staging a property before it goes to market.

Why does staging work?

Staging costs less than a price reduction.

According to a National Association of Realtors survey, homes that sold after four weeks on the market sold for 6% less than ones within the first four weeks! Staging your vacant home more than pays for the risk of having to reduce your selling price.

You will make more money.

U.S. Housing and Urban Development reports that a staged home will sell, on average, 17% higher than an unstaged home.

Your house will sell faster, which means less hassle.

The New York Village Voice reported that the average number of days an unstaged home is on the market is 17 more than a staged home. See their report.
